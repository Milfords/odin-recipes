Beef Stew

Description

This recipe is a delicious iteration of beef stew that is mainly hands off. It takes a bit of prep work, but it will roast in the slow cooker while at work, running errands, or any other activities that you need to do throughout the day. This home cooked meal is perfect for a family that is constantly on the go is looking for something to taste amazing without a ton of work put in.

The prep time for this recipe is about 20 minutes. The cook time is about 4 hours bringing the total time up to 4 hours and 20 minutes until the food is completely ready. It should have about 6 servings if all steps are completed correctly in the recipe and contains 576 calories, 44 grams of protein, and 30 grams of carbohydrates in each serving.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ cups beef broth
  • 4 medium carrots, sliced
  • 3 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 large bay leaf

Steps

  1. Place meat in slow cooker.
  2. Mix flour, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl. Pour over meat, and stir until meat is coated.
  3. Add beef broth, carrots, potatoes, onion, celery, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, garlic, and bay leave; stir to combine.
  4. Cover, and cook until beef is tender enough to cut with a spoon, on Low for 8 to 12 hours, or on High for 4 to 6 hours.
